Shipping

UK

Shipping method is at my discretion, but under normal circumstances I ship by Royal Mail, First Class post.

I generate my shipping information from the information you provide, so please ensure you provide full and correct name and address details.

Once payment has been cleared, I am normally able to ship your order within 5 - 10 working days.

Please note there may be the occasional production delay which may result in an extra day or two to ship your order. If there is likely to be a delay in shipping your order, I will advise you by email at the earliest opportunity.

Overseas Shipping

We are happy to ship our products worldwide, however, it should be noted that the maximum weight is 2kg per package for standard airmail. Anything over 2kg would be shipped by Parcelforce Worldwide. Shipping costs would be advised when overall weight is known.

As the costs can add a large chunk to your overall order value, especially on larger orders the table below should give you an idea what you can expect to pay for delivery of your order.

Under normal circumstances, I ship through Royal Mail using standard Airmail. However, this is not a tracked system, and delivery can take between 10 and 14 working days to arrive.

As an alternative, I use the Royal Mail 'Airsure' service. This is a tracked service and delivery is usually a little faster, though this service does cost extra. The chart below shows current Royal Mail shipping costs for standard Airmail and Airsure options.
