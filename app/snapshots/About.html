About Battlescene Designs

Welcome to Battlescene Designs.

We are a sole trader company set up after many years of making our own model scenery for various wargame systems.

Originally, I only intended to make 'one off' individual pieces of scenery for sale, but decided to go down the line of sculpting, moulding and casting each piece for sale instead.

As gamers ourselves, we also believe that wargaming should be as affordable as possible. We design all of our models so as to enable our margin to be as low as possible, hence allowing us to sell our models at as low a price as possible.

Here you will find the start of the Battlescene Designs range which I'm sure will grow quite rapidly, so please keep watching for new products appearing on this site.

I hope you enjoy!

Paul

PS. All of our products (unless stated otherwise) come unpainted and un-assembled. Due to the nature of the resin hand-casting process we use and the intricate detail on our items, there is a possibility that a few small defects may be apparent in the final casts. The nature of these will be in the form of tiny air bubbles or mould lines/flashing. Unfortunately this cannot be helped but if any significant bubbling occurs, it will be repaired before being dispatched. These repairs will be in the form of Green Stuff or Miliput.
